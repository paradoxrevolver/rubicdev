me

my name is rubic, paradoxrevolver, or you can call me adam.

this website is rubic.dev, home to all the projects and parts i've worked on over the years.

i'm an artist, programmer, and game designer that enjoys creating useful tools and clever games. i'm currently a student at San Jose State University graduating in fall 2020 with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering and a minor in Mathematics.

my goal is to find a place in game development where i can write code, draw art, and design experiences that inspire others.

art

i started drawing in 2014. while more of my focus over the years have been directed towards programming, i still occasionally return to drawing or modeling just to remember how to use a pen.

the main tools i use for art includes Paint Tool SAI, Paint.NET, Aseprite, Illustrator, and Blender.
