me

my name is rubic, paradoxrevolver, or you can call me adam.

this website is rubic.dev, home to all the projects and parts i've worked on over the years.

i'm an artist, programmer, and game designer that enjoys creating useful tools and clever games. i'm currently a student at San Jose State University graduating in fall of 2020 with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering and a minor in Mathematics.

my goal is to find a place in game development where i can write code, draw art, and design experiences that inspire others.
