ABOUT ME

My name is rubic, or you can call me Adam.

This website is rubic.dev, home to all the projects and parts I've worked on over the years.

I'm an artist, a programmer, and a game designer. I like making clever tools and games!

I graduated summa cum laude from San Jose State University with a BS in Software Engineering and a minor in Mathematics.
